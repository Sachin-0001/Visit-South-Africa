Bobotie

A classic dish made of spiced minced meat baked with an egg-based topping.

Dive into the comforting embrace of Bobotie, a fusion of sweet and savory flavors that pays homage to the culinary traditions of South Africa. The warmth of the spices, combined with a creamy topping, creates a dish that feels like home, inviting you to savor every bite.

Bunny Chow

A street food favorite from Durban consisting of a hollowed-out loaf of bread filled with curry.

Experience the vibrant streets of Durban with Bunny Chow, where spicy curry meets soft, pillowy bread. This handheld delight offers a burst of flavor in every bite, a true testament to the rich culinary tapestry of South Africa.
